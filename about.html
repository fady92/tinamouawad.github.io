Welcome to the Turtle Girl!


Why Turtle Girl? Think about the Rabbit and Turtle tale. Consistency is my strength, and as you can tell I am not the fastest out there (a positive way to say that I'm relatively slow ).

My name is Antonia Mouawad, 22 years old, currently completing my masters in computer science at the University of Toronto. Not going to bug you with academics, don't worry.

I started running to lose some weight in 2016 and guess what? A year later, running was my new lifestyle! I signed up for my first 10k race that year, then my second. After that, my best friend signed me up for 542, an amazing running training program in Lebanon and my brother got me registered for my first half marathon in October.

The goal of the current challenge is to give my training a deeper meaning by raising awareness against child abuse, and funds for """" that supports this goal. I will be running irrespective of the weather- which can be very challenging in Toronto- and of my schedule, 7 km every day for a full month. I will be posting my running logs daily, along with a paragraph/video/drawing (yes I draw as well) to come one step closer to protecting the children from abuse. It's time to make every step count! Support me by donating. Everyone can make a difference.
